emma email

Beautiful email, thoughtfully crafted for everyday people.

Email's gotten out of hand. Your inbox should make you feel good. Fellow travelers missing Newton will be pleased.

Beautiful

Inboxes are like messy closets—overwhelming and scary to open. Emma organizes them with thoughtful clarity and visual calm. Go ahead and leave the door open. It’s beautiful inside.

Private

Emma securely transfers your emails from Gmail's servers to the device in your hand. Then she forgets about them. Your emails are never persisted by Emma in any way. They belong to you.

Assertive

Unsubscribing should take a single tap—requiring any more effort is dishonest. Emma auto-blocks senders who try and force a sign-in or preference adjustment to unsubscribe. It's your inbox.

Thoughtful

When you're emailed a two factor authentication code, Emma forwards it to you in a text message so that you can tap that "autofill from text message" option. It's just easier.
